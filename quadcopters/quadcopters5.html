Information about the UDI Free Loop Quadcopter :-

This mini quadcopter is easy to fly and it features a 180° flip flight function and a high precision 6-axis gyro stabilization system providing a wonderful flight performance, which is perfect for both beginners and advanced pilots alike.

  • With headless flight mode
  • This quadcopter can perform 180° flip in any direction.
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Small size, can fit in the palm of your hand
  • 2.4GHz 4-channel full function radio transmitter (Mode 2)
  • High precision 6-axis gyro stabilization gives you super stable flight
  • Equipped with protective frame to reduce damages due to crash
