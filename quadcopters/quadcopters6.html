Information about the XK-X250-X350 Quadcopter :-

The top model comes with preinstalled 2D gimbal and 1080P full HD camera. Features like super headless mode, circle hovering, auto return to home and an incredible 30 minutes of flight time makes this quadcopter very interesting for those who are looking for a cheap alternative to the Apollo.

  • 8520 coreless motors;
  • Compatible with Futaba S-FHSS transmitters
  • Flight time: up to 12 minutes
  • Flight distance: up to 300 meters
  • 3.7V 800mAh Li-Po battery
  • Low voltage battery alarm
  • HK X250 total weight: 100g
